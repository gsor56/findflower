FindFlower is an identification aid

FindFlower helps you recognise flowers from a photograph and learn about them. It is a supportive tool for curiosity and learning — a starting point for understanding the plant life around you, not a certified botanical authority.

By using FindFlower, you agree to these terms. If you do not agree, please don’t use the product.

Predictions may be wrong

Machine identification is probabilistic. Results are a best estimate, expressed with a confidence score and a set of alternatives precisely because they are not guaranteed to be correct.

The current Beta model recognises a limited set of botanical classes. A flower outside that set may be misidentified or matched to the closest thing the model knows. Treat every result as a considered suggestion to verify, not a fact.

You are responsible for how you act on FindFlower’s output. Because a mistaken identification can have real consequences, you agree not to rely on it for any decision affecting health or safety — including whether a plant is safe to touch, eat, or use — without independent, expert confirmation.
