Common questions

Is FindFlower really free right now?

Yes. Throughout the Beta, identifying flowers in your browser is completely free, with no account required.

When will prices be set?

Pricing is being finalised alongside the hosted API. We’ll announce it clearly here and give existing users advance notice before anything changes.

Do I need a plan to use the on-device SDK?

No. The on-device SDK runs the model in your own app with no key and no per-request cost. Paid plans apply to the hosted API and team features. See the API page.
